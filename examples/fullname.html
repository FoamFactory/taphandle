Full Name Field Examples

Note

For all examples, the prefix used is ninkasi.

The following is an example of how to use Taphandle with a field for validation that expects to have a user's full (first and last) name. A full name field is defined as an <input> tag with type="text" and a class of PREFIX_fullNameField. Note that "Full Names" are names with at least two words that may include suffixes such as "Jr.", "Sr.", and "III", but not numerals (e.g. "3").
